Welcome to A Coach Called Life

Discover your inner strength

Hi, I'm Swamy. My core belief is that Life is a great coach. It teaches us through our own experience. Most of the time, the lessons we get in life can be mistaken for adversities. However, these difficulties that we once considered as problems to our existence reveal themselves to be great teachers with the right support and direction. Whether you are experiencing intense anxiety, panic, feelings of unreality, depersonalization or derealization, I can help you understand what is happening to you. And with that understanding you will discover your inner strength to confront your fears and live the life you desire.

Find out more
Living with anxiety can always be frustrating since people don't usually understand what it is like to be with. So many times in the past I've wished I had someone to talk to when I was going through a tough time. I want that help to be able to available to you. I'm offereing a free one hour session either through Skype or in person. Tell me what you are going through and we can find out if we share work something out.

You

You are tired of fighting your anxiety and panic. You have a life waiting to be lived but your anxiety is in between the life you have right now and the life you want to live. Despite being strong and capable, you are looking for guidance on how to manage your anxiety. You are determined to live your life without worries.

Me

I am here to fully support your growth. I consider it my calling and purpose to help others get unstuck in the flow of life. I have had a transformational last 10 years. Freeing myself from the fetters of anxiety and feelings of un-reality,  I learned how to better myself physically, emotionally and spiritually and walk on a path of personal development. With all of that knowledge and experience, I want to help you grow inside your own cocoon of transformation and emerge as a luminous butterfly.

Our Work

This is no one size fits all approach. We are going to get specific with you, your life and your challenges. They are unique to you. We will employ creative methods to go deep inside of you. This means our sessions will usually go beyond just talking about problems. With a focus on wholeness we will be using your own deep intuition, your feelings and your body sensations as a guide to shed light on aspects of yourself that might be holding you back.
