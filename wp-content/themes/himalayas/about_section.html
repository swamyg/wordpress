Welcome to A Coach Called Life

Discover your inner strength

Hi, I'm Swamy and my core belief is that Life is a great coach. It teaches us through our own experience. Most of the time, the lessons we endure in life can be mistaken for adversities. However, these difficulties that we once considered as problems to our existence reveal themselves to be great teachers with the right support and direction. If you are experiencing intense anxiety, panic, feelings of unreality, depersonalization or derealization, I can help you to understand what is happening to you. And with that understanding, you will discover your inner strength to confront your fears and live the life you desire.
