Welcome to A Coach Called Life

Discover your inner strength

Hi, I'm Swamy and my core belief is that Life is a great coach. It shows you where to go next through your own feelings. Without a clear understanding or guidance, we end up running away from these feelings instead of turning inward. If you are experiencing intense anxiety, panic, feelings of unreality, depersonalization or derealization, I can help you to understand what is happening to you. And with that understanding, you will discover your inner strength to confront your fears and live the life you desire.
